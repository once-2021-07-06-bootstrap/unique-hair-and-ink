Meet The Staff

Richmond Barber

Richmond is passionate about business and cutting hair, he graduated from Texas Barber College in 2011. He started his career in Mankato at Y Barbers in 2014.  Northside Hair Co was opened with his wife Jennifer in 2018 in North Mankato. He loves to travel, participate in community activities and spend time with his family.   He is a father of 7 children (4 girls and 3 boys).  He specializes in straight razor shaves.

Charlie Barber

Charlie is a Mankato native graduating from Mankato West High School in 2015. Attending Moler Barber School in 2016. He started his career at Y barbers following graduation from Moler Barber School.  He  relocated  to Northside Hair Co. in 2018. He loves to fish and spend time with his family. He is a father of 2 boys.  He specializes in designs and fades.

Michael Tattoo Apprentice

Michael has always had an interest in art, including street art, graffiti and painting. He first picked up his tattoo machine in 2011 and fell in love with the permanent art form .  He enjoys spending time with his family and friends. He strives to stay ahead of the game with new techniques so he can apply them to his art.  He wants every client he sees to have an outstanding experience.
